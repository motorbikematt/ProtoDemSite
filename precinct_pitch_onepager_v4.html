The mechanism

Evidence in. Accountability out.

📄

01 — Ingest

AI ingests official records — votes, minutes, agendas, bills — across local, state, and federal tiers. Surfaces gaps between stated positions and official actions.

🗂️

02 — Arm

Captain receives source-verified contradiction cards: what the official said, what the record shows, and the citation. Ready to share in thirty seconds.

🤝

03 — Engage

Captain shows the gap to a neighbor — not a stranger, an elected representative they already know. Credibility does the work that cold outreach can't.

📞

04 — Act

Voter gets a one-tap action: call script, email template, public comment window, or meeting time. Officials respond — or their record shows they didn't.

The objection answered

Why voters will engage

"Voters don't show up. Why would they call an official over a digest?"

They won't — for a generic newsletter. They will when their neighbor hands them a specific contradiction and a direct action step. The captain isn't a brand; they're an elected person from the same three streets. Credibility is the mechanism. The information alone doesn't move people. The information plus a trusted face plus a clear next step does. That combination has never been systematically delivered before.

Scope

All three tiers held accountable

One captain. Every layer of government that touches their neighbors' lives.

Local City council, school board, township trustees — the officials closest to daily life, least watched.
State State legislators — votes on education, labor, and healthcare that drive most local outcomes.
Federal Congressional delegation — positioned votes and constituent service records, not just soundbites.

Accountability > transparency. Transparency gives voters raw information. Accountability gives them a specific contradiction, a credible messenger, and a path to response. Precinct delivers all three — every cycle, every tier.

What we build

Three layers that didn't exist before

1 Record ingestion engine — continuous ingest of official documents across all three tiers. LLM-structured, source-linked, jurisdiction-resolved.
2 Captain publishing interface — contradiction cards, digest drafts, and action-step templates ready to edit and push in one workflow. No research required.
3 Constituent response layer — subscribers read, react, and act. Response data becomes geo-tagged engagement intelligence for the party — and a paper trail for officials.
